HELLO AND WELCOME

My name is Katie Situ and I am a versatile designer and photographer. I currently reside in Ottawa, Canada where I study Graphic Design. As a designer with many interests within the art industry, I strive to be as versatile as possible. I love to learn and discover. I am open-minded and always up for a challenge. Graphic Design is my passion and Photography is my peace, but I will always strive to be great at both.

XANDER SITU / LOGO DESIGN

A grunge-street style design to represent Ottawa-based DJ, Xander Situ. The design was based on a certain style with paint splats to create a grunge feel. The logo design can be used for promotional merchandising and event posters.

BLACK NINJAS / PRESS KIT

Black Ninjas is an Ottawa-based DJ duo. The press kit design offers a minimalistic design that suits the duo's style. This consists of an audio sample, press photos, biography, resume, and business cards, all in one. All contents will be within the vinyl style kit that allows for a simple and minimalistic function and design.

INSTRUMENTOR / UI APP DESIGN

Instrumentor a is digital mentoring app that lets growing musicians learn to play musical instruments. The design consists of strong and bold colours that hold a sense of motivation with its powerful palette. The design elements are very modern and minimalistic. Together, it creates a refreshingly daring personality that targets an older audience.

VISUAL MEMORIES / PHOTOBOOK

Visual Memories is a photography book that showcases my own photography. It will include a short introduction of each album and multiple photos of the album. The photos will include portrait, landscape, and nature.
